NeuroCare is a smart and AI-powered web platform designed to assist in the early detection of brain tumors using MRI images. Our goal is to bridge the gap between patients and early diagnosis by providing fast, accurate, and accessible predictions.

Why NeuroCare?

Early detection of brain tumors is critical for successful treatment. However, access to neurologists and diagnostic tools can be limited. BrainScan uses deep learning models trained on MRI datasets to detect brain tumors with high accuracy in just a few seconds.

  • AI-powered detection system
  • Quick MRI scan analysis
  • User-friendly interface
  • No doctor consultation needed for initial checks

💻 Technology Stack

Frontend HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Tailwind CSS
Backend Django (Python)
AI Model TensorFlow + VGG16
Database SQLite
